About Lakbay

We Don't Just Book Trips.
We Craft Journeys.

Lakbay (Filipino for journey) was founded in 2018 by three friends who were tired of generic travel packages that missed what makes the Philippines truly magical — the warmth of the people, the hidden coves only locals know, and the stories woven into every island.

Today, we are a DOT-accredited full-service travel agency offering flights, island tours, cultural experiences, and hotel bookings across all 7,641 islands. Every package is personally curated by our team of Filipino travel experts.
